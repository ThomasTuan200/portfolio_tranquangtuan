About me

I am introverted and stoic person. I have learned a lesson that sometime you need to take actions to gain more experience and to enrich my perspective. During those journey, I find myself that I love Graphic Design and I want to design a product that could help other convey their message in a smart way.

I am learning at multimedia school known as ARENA MULTIMEDIA. I have learned and sharpened many basic to advanced techniques in Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, and Logo Designing during my first semester. However, during my semester 2, I had discovered another passion in Web Designing and how things coded are brought into real product. Therefore, I am learning how to do Web Design in semester two and my upcoming goal is to become a proficient UX/UI Designer.

My specialties are designing Point Of Sales Materials, Stationary Design, Logo Design and Branding Guideline projects.

"I always try my best to achieve the best result!"
